Stellar

Stellar is an open-source program inspired in 'Game Maker' for Pygame/Python development.

The goal is to have a program to design your own games using easy-to-learn drag-and-drop actions and different easy tools for begginers.
When you become more experienced, you will have the possibility of writing and editing your game with the full flexibility given by Python/Pygame.

This is an incomplete version. Currently, it has almost nothing, but I would love to be helped by anyone interested in the project.

You are free to distribute the games you create with Stellar in any way you like. You can even sell them.
This of course assumes that the sprites, images, and sounds you use can be distributed or sold as well.
